How To Reach Us


DIRECTIONS TO REACH IIIT RAICHUR CAMPUS at GEC Raichur :-

All the activities of IIIT Raichur will be running in new temporary campus at the Government Engineering College, Raichur.

GEC Raichur: It is located at Yeramarus which is 10 Kilometers away from Raichur city.



FROM RAICHUR CITY :-

From Raichur Bus Station to GEC Raichur
Bus Stand Road, Androon Quilla, Raichur, Karnataka.

+ Take a local bus at Raichur bus station towards Sagamkunta, D. Rampur. The Journey takes about 15 min from Raichur Bus station.

+ Bus station is about 7.2 Km from Government Engineering College Raichur.

+ At Raichur Bus Station Catch local bus towards Sagamkunta and D. Rampur (Raichur bus Station-Ambedkar circle stop-Basweshwara circle stop-Gung circle stop-RIMS-Yeramarus Camp-I Stop-SLN College of Engineering.)

+ GEC Bus stop will come before Yeramarus Camp I.

NOTE :-

Autos are also available from Raichur Bus Station to GEC Raichur.

($) Bus Charge approximately – 10/-

($) Auto Charges approximately- 80/-



From Raichur Railway Station to GEC Raichur

+ Autos are available from Raichur Railway station to Bus Stop.

+ Local autos are available from Raichur Railway station to GEC Raichur.

+ Auto Charges from Raichur Railway station to GEC Raichur Approx. 90/-

+ Local buses are available from Raichur bus station towards Sagamkunta and D. Rampur which will reach GEC Raichur within 20 min.

+ Distance from Railway station to GEC Raichur is Approximately 8.5 KM.



FROM HYDERABAD CITY :-

By Bus from Mahatma Gandhi bus station (MGBS)

+ Take a bus towards Raichur from terminal no. 30 at Mahatma Gandhi bus station (MGBS) Hyderabad (Ticket charges Approx Rs.220/- )

+ Buses are available after every 30 min from MGBS.

+ Distance from MGBS to GEC Raichur is approx. 200 km.


By Train from Secunderabad railway station

+ Take a train from Secunderabad railway station towards Raichur.

+ Distance from Secunderabad Railway station to Raichur Railway station is approx. 220 km.

+ The Journey takes approximately 5 hrs from Secunderabad Railway Station to Raichur Railway station.

+ Ticket charges Rs.125/- (General ticket fair) from Secunderabad Railway station to Raichur Railway station.

FROM RAJIV GANDHI INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT HYDERABAD :-

Authorized Taxis are available from RGI Airport to GEC Raichur. Taxi fare approx (Rs.3500 – Rs.5000) depending on time.

OR

Take a Cab from RGI Airport to Mahatma Gandhi bus station (MGBS) Hyderabad, Catch interstate bus towards Raichur at terminal no 30 at Mahatma Gandhi bus station. (Ticket charges Approx Rs.220/- )

OR

Take a Cab from RGI Airport to Secunderabad railway station, Catch the trains towards Raichur.
